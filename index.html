Dan Friese

I graduated Summa Cum Laude from Oregon State University in March 2026 with a B.S. in Geography & Geospatial Science, a GIS Certificate, and an Earth Sciences minor.

As a Research Assistant at Oregon State University and CHARTER Fellow with the Cascadia Coastlines and Peoples Hub, I produced prototype multi-hazard evacuation maps for Lincoln County, facilitated community mapping workshops, and conducted spatial analysis on coastal community assets survey data. My CHARTER research, presented at the 2025 Pacific Sociological Association conference in San Francisco, focused on tsunami hazard exposure and demographic vulnerability in Clatsop County.

As a Cartographer for the University of Oregon, I have produced multi-hazard planning map series for Marion and Curry Counties and energy infrastructure maps for the Oregon Department of Energy.

PROJECT 01

Multi-Hazard Evacuation Maps

Cascadia Coastlines & Peoples Hub · Lincoln County, Oregon · 2024–25

Designed prototype multi-hazard evacuation maps for Lincoln County coastal communities. Maps served as study materials in NSF-funded research on hazard map effectiveness, using a pre/post assessment design with map activities that compared prototype maps against existing DOGAMI maps.

ArcGIS Pro Evacuation Mapping Spanish Edition Community Engagement Emergency Mgmt NSF Research
PROJECT 02

Marion County Hazard Planning Maps

University of Oregon · Marion County, Oregon · 2024–25

A 62-map hazard planning series for Marion County covering six planning areas. Separate maps for evacuation routes, individual hazards, population density, and wildfire footprints. Produced in coordination with county officials.

ArcGIS Pro Evacuation Hazard Planning Multi-Hazard

Oregon Dept. of Energy: Infrastructure Maps

University of Oregon · Malheur County · 2025

Energy infrastructure mapping for the Oregon Department of Energy. Layered energy facility, transmission line, and substation data with hazard vulnerability overlays to produce county-level risk summaries.

Energy Infrastructure Hazard Overlay Spatial Analysis State Agency ArcGIS Pro
