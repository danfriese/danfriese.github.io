PROJECT 01

Multi-Hazard Evacuation Maps

CoPes Hub NSF · Lincoln County, Oregon · 2024–25

Participatory pilot cartographic series covering Newport, Lincoln City, Toledo, Depoe Bay, and Waldport. Each map overlays earthquake, tsunami, wildfire, and flood hazards with evacuation routes, developed through an iterative public engagement process. Coordinated five community workshops with the general public, Latine coastal residents, and high school students — incorporating pre/post assessments and group discussion with local emergency managers and DOGAMI. Results showed increased self-efficacy and reduced misconceptions about tsunami hazards. Spanish-language editions produced in collaboration with bilingual team members. Work is being prepared for submission to a cartography journal.

ArcGIS Pro Multi-Hazard Bilingual Community Engagement Emergency Mgmt NSF Research
PROJECT 02

Marion County Multi-Hazard Atlas

UO Contract · Post-2020 Labor Day Fires · 2024–25

Full-county map atlas motivating by the devastating 2020 Labor Day fires. Six planning areas, each with evacuation maps, hazard overlays (wildfire, earthquake, flooding, landslide, liquefaction), population density maps, and planning area boundaries. Delivered to county emergency management.

Evacuation QGIS County Atlas 6 Hazard Types Contract Work

Oregon Dept. of Energy — Infrastructure Mapping

Energy infrastructure atlases for two Oregon counties, mapping production facilities, transmission lines, substations, and utility providers layered with multi-hazard vulnerability data (wildfire burn probability, earthquake shaking, flooding, landslides, tsunami inundation).

Oregon State University · 2025

PROJECT 06

Healthcare Deserts in Oregon — R Spatial Analysis

GEOG 361 · Oregon State University · 2024

R-based spatial analysis mapping healthcare access across Oregon census block groups. Applied population-weighted distance modeling, Pearson correlation with census income data, and Global/Local Moran's I spatial autocorrelation to identify underserved communities. Produced with sf, ggplot2, ggspatial, tidycensus, and tidyverse in R Markdown.

R / ggplot2 sf tidycensus Moran's I Census Data R Markdown
